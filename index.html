Watch for your friend's activities

Imagine that you have friends who enjoy playing in different realms and for both factions (for the Horde and for the Alliance). Maybe their characters even have the same name. How do you track them and easily know when a certain character is online on your server and in your faction?

This simple open source addon will allow you to select friends you want to track and it will always show your friends' current faction in Friends List window. After selecting friends to track, as soon as they login to another character, this addon will inform you which character your friend is currently playing, in what realm, and for which faction:

[Chuck Norris] (Ubermegakiller-Stormrage) has entered game world for the Alliance.

You can find this addon on curse.com website, or download it directly from here and install it manually by placing the AddOn folder in your World of Warcraft's/Interface/AddOns directory.
