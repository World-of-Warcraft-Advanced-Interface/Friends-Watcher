Watch for your friend's activity

Imagine that you have friends who enjoy playing on different realms and for both factions (for the Horde and for the Alliance), and maybe even their characters got same names. How you'll track them and easily understand when special character is online on your server and for your faction?

This simple open source addon will let you to select witch friends you want to track and it will always show current friend's factions in Friends List window. After selecting some of your friends, as soon as your riends will login to another character, this addon will inform you, for witch character your friend is currently playing, on what realm and for what faction:

[Chuck Norris] (Ubermegakiller-Stormrage) has entered game world for the Alliance.

You can find this addon on curse.com website, or download it directly from here and manually install it, by placing AddOn folder in to your World of Warcraft's Interface directory.
